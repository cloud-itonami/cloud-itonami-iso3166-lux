# Business Model — Grand Duchy of Luxembourg

## Offer
- Portail des marchés publics (pmp.lu / marches.public.lu) — public
  procurement portal, issued/administered under the Ministre de la
  Mobilité et des Travaux publics (Loi du 8 avril 2018 sur les marchés
  publics + Règlement grand-ducal du 8 avril 2018, as amended 29 mai
  2024)
- Registre de Commerce et des Sociétés (RCS, operated by Luxembourg
  Business Registers) + TVA registration (Administration de
  l'enregistrement, des domaines et de la TVA)
- Autorisation d'établissement (business license, Loi modifiée du 2
  septembre 2011) — final issuance sequentially requires the RCS record
  above to already exist
- EU member — no national-content quota; EU single-market access

## Trust Controls
- `:filing/submit` never automated
- fabricated claims are HARD holds
- autorisation-d'établissement conditions (Loi du 2 septembre 2011 Arts.
  3-5) are independently recomputed, never taken on the advisor's word
- the restricted-procedure-without-publication ceiling (currently 79 000
  EUR, Art. 20 + RGD Art. 151 as amended 29 mai 2024) is independently
  recomputed against the CURRENT amended text, not a stale figure
